About me

I am a Research Analyst at the Flatiron Institute in the Systems Biology group (Bonneau Lab) in the Center for Computational Biology in New York, New York. I apply network science and machine learning to research problems relating to protein structure and function.

In the past, I have done research in methods for crowdsourcing and applications for natural language understanding and computer vision. In general, I am interested in interpreting complex systems with advanced computational methods.
